WHAT MOTIVATE ME DURING ONLINE LEARNING

1. Self-Motivation

I always remind myself that no matter how hard it is to follow the classes,
I can do it and I can strive for it


2. The online learning environment

The environment really give me a lot of motivation,
if the environment is not active,
I will get really sleepy
meanwhile, if the environment is so active,
I will give my 100% attention during the class.


3. The online learning process

I will be really excited to join classes if whenever I ask question
I get the feedback and if I write a wrong answer,
the right answer will be given immediately.


4. I think about the reasons why I continue my study

Whenever I feel like online learning is too hard
and I feel like I want giveup,
I always think the main reason why I choose to continue my study.
